About the Sport

The Hunger Games involves 12 districts that will choose a competitor, also known as a "Tribute", to compete in the sport. The "tribute" can either be a male or a female. This only rule this sport has is "Last one standing wins". It is televised nationally and the "Tributes" are to fight against one another with whatever weapons they have and survive until one remaining "Tribute" is left standing. The motto of this sport is "May The Odds Be In Your Favour".
